Bacteriology
Master the Gram stain first — it halves your differential instantly. Then layer virulence factors, clinical syndromes, and treatment. High-yield sequences: cocci → rods; GP → GN.
🧬
Virology
Group by genome type: RNA vs DNA, ss vs ds, +sense vs −sense. For each virus know its tropism and distinguishing lab findings.
🪱
Parasitology
Organize by habitat: intestinal → blood/tissue. Vector associations (mosquito/tick) are heavily tested. Eosinophilia = helminth migration.
🍄
Mycology
Divide into systemic dimorphic fungi vs opportunistic. Dimorphic rule: mold in cold, yeast in heat. Geography matters.
